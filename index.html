About Us

About MES

The Mahatma Education Society embarked upon its mission of "Education for all" l in the year 1970.The vision, dedication, global outlook, tenacious struggle and undaunted spirit of the Chairman and C.E.O., Dr. K. M. Vasudevan Pillai and the forward looking, untiring energy of the Secretary, Dr. (Mrs.) Daphne Pillai have now transformed the Mahatma Education Society in to a vast educational organization. Pillai HOC College of Engineering and Technology at Rasayani has state-of-the-art facilities and distinguished faculty who have been a nurturing ground for students of high academic capabilities. It is established on a 14 acre of lush green campus with more than 3,00,000 sq. ft of built up area comprising of spacious classrooms, well-equipped laboratories and workshops, new age computer facilities and a well-stocked library which provide a stimulating educational environment within the college. PHCET is housed in an eleven storied building. The College has attracted qualified and experienced faculty members and has been constantly adding experienced faculty members to its already growing number of about 120 teaching staff.

About Tech Euforia

Tech-Euforia is a national level technical festival of Pillai HOC College of Engineering and Technology. This technical fest has become one of the largest collegiate festivals in India attracting a footfall of over 10,000 people every year. It’s an initiative made to ignite the aspiring minds, thus encouraging students for the brighter tomorrow. This fest is a perfect blend of many workshops, technical and fun events providing platform for showcasing innovation in the field of science and technology. Tech-Euforia will once again bring together the smartest minds from all across to showcase their skills by being a part of events and workshops. It has thus set a line of success, boasting events and workshops that will get bigger and better every year.

Our Theme: Master Pool

Tech-Euforia-where imagination meets innovation; this year we present the theme the ‘Master Pool’, dedicated to all the great minds that inspired the world with the great ideas and work. Master Pool-give our participants a pool of opportunities to touch all chords of this vibrant environment where experiences are shared and achievements are showcased. With each growing year our fest is becoming bigger and better with polished calibre and competence. Events are organized and managed to bring out the best in you by awakening your inner master. Come and join this inspirational fest from February 15th to 18th wherein your friends from various colleges to experience talent, fun and life.
